Carrom

  • Striker must be within the baseline.
  • You must pocket your color coins first.
  • Queen must be covered by your coin.
  • Avoid fouls to prevent penalty.
  • Proper turn order must be followed.

Chess

  • Each player starts with 16 pieces.
  • King must be protected from checkmate.
  • Pawn moves forward but captures diagonally.
  • Knight moves in an L-shape.
  • Game ends in checkmate or stalemate.

Badminton

  • A match consists of best-of-three games.
  • The shuttle must not touch the ground.
  • Players must serve diagonally.
  • Net contact results in a fault.
  • Each game is played to 21 points.

Box Cricket

  • Each team gets limited overs.
  • Direct hitting on the side walls is not allowed.
  • Overarm bowling is not permitted.
  • Batsmen must run between wickets.
  • Caught out on direct ceiling hit.

100m Race

  • Sprinters must stay in their lanes.
  • False start results in disqualification.
  • Race ends when chest crosses the finish line.
  • No pushing or obstruction allowed.
  • Wind assistance limit is 2.0 m/s.

Football

  • Match lasts 90 minutes with a halftime.
  • Only goalkeeper can touch the ball with hands.
  • Offside rule applies to attackers.
  • A foul results in a free kick or penalty.
  • A red card means immediate dismissal.

Hockey

  • Match is played in four quarters.
  • Ball must be hit with the flat side of the stick.
  • No physical contact allowed.
  • Penalty corner given for defensive fouls.
  • Goals must be scored from inside the D.

Kho-Kho

  • Chasing team must tag opponents to eliminate them.
  • Defenders must not cross the center line.
  • Runners can dodge and avoid being tagged.
  • Each team gets 9 minutes to chase.
  • Kho must be given properly to teammates.

Kabaddi

  • Raider must chant ‘Kabaddi’ continuously.
  • Raider must touch at least one opponent to score.
  • Defenders can stop the raider using tackles.
  • Each raid lasts a maximum of 30 seconds.
  • Team with most points at the end wins.

Tug of War

  • Each team must pull the rope towards their side.
  • A central mark decides the winner.
  • Feet must remain planted during the pull.
  • No sudden jerks are allowed.
  • Best of three rounds decides the winner.

Relay

  • Each team consists of 4 runners.
  • Baton exchange must happen within the zone.
  • Dropping the baton leads to disqualification.
  • Runners must stay in their lane.
  • Fastest team wins the race.

Volleyball

  • Each team has 6 players on the court.
  • Ball can be hit a maximum of three times before returning.
  • Players must rotate positions.
  • Ball must not touch the ground.
  • A team needs 25 points to win a set.
